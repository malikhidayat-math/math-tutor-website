About Me

Making complex Mathematics feel simple

I'm Hidayat ul Allah, an online Mathematics tutor with a genuine love for the subject and, just as importantly, for teaching it well. My academic path — an M.Phil in Mathematics, an MBA, and an M.A in Education — has given me both deep command of mathematical theory and a trained understanding of how students actually learn.

Over the years I've noticed the same pattern again and again: students don't dislike Mathematics, they dislike feeling lost in it. My approach is to slow down at exactly the point where confusion usually begins, rebuild the concept from first principles, and let the student reach the "aha" moment themselves rather than simply handing them a formula. Whether it's a school student meeting Algebra for the first time or a university student wrestling with Differential Equations, the method is the same: clarity first, speed second.

I teach students internationally through live, interactive online sessions, combining structured lesson plans with real problem-solving practice, regular feedback, and a genuinely patient, encouraging teaching style. My goal in every class is simple — help each student leave a little more confident than they arrived.
